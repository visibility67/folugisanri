What exactly is AEO schema markup — in plain English?
+

Think of it as a label on the back of your article that only AI tools can read. A regular blog post tells Google there are words on a page. AEO schema markup tells ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI: "This article is about HVAC repair in Maple Grove, Minnesota, written by a licensed local contractor."

Without it, AI tools have to guess what your content is about — and they usually skip it. With it, they can confidently recommend your company when a homeowner asks for a contractor in your city. 88% of contractor websites have zero schema markup. That's the gap.
